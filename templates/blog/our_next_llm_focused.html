Large Language Models are rapidly becoming the interface of work. They let teams talk to data, tools, and workflows; combine institutional knowledge with real-time signals through retrieval and tool use; and move from prototype to production in days—not months.

Our next focused LLM bootcamp lands in Erbil this January. It is an in-person, two-day experience designed for builders who want to ship operational AI systems that stand up in the real world. We start each morning with crisp concepts, spend afternoons in build labs, and end with a mentored showcase so you leave with a working asset in hand.

What to expect during the two days

We warm up with a lightweight coding icebreaker before diving into modular product design, advanced SQL and database orchestration, server deployment, and data visualisation. From there we move into machine-learning prediction patterns and hands-on operational LLM tactics: prompt design, retrieval, evaluation, and multi-agent automation.

Each participant ships a capstone project with a mentor on hand. You will walk away with a production-ready repo, a hosted demo, reusable templates, and a certificate documenting the experience.

Why Erbil?

Erbil is a fast-growing hub with a deep talent pool in energy, public services, and entrepreneurship. By running the bootcamp face-to-face in the city, we can pair regional context with global best practices so teams can test and launch AI capabilities that matter locally. If your organisation would like to host, reach out—we are finalising a modern, central venue with excellent connectivity.

Bring your questions

Every cohort includes a prompt-thinking quiz to sharpen intuition. Try this one— what is the difference in the outputs of these two prompts when you run them in a creative image model? Reply with your take and we will feature a few sharp answers in the next letter.
